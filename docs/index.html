Hey, I'm
Sam Bai

An enthusiastic programmer and lifelong learner. Currently designing user-interfaces that promote automation and accessibility of information and data. Always looking for new ways to connect with people.


PROJECTS

One of the many joys of having an interest in software engineering/development is working on my fair share of technical projects. These range from classic arcade games to data analysis and visualizations to this website you're looking at right now!

My philosophy is that a programmer should be well-rounded and adept in multiple technologies and areas. Computer science is no longer just one thing anymore; the cross-section between different fields keeps expanding. Feel free to take a look at some of the projects that I've done over the years as I continue to learn more about the computing world we live in.

ART

At a young age, I was incredibly passionate about art. I remember in elementary school, I got a box of colored markers for my birthday and it was one of the most exciting gifts I'd ever received.

Today, art still remains as one of my favorite hobbies. From printmaking to digital art to photography, I find different ways to connect with art. Feel free to take a look at some of the pieces that I'm most proud of.

BLOG

In Chinese culture, we're always taught to internalize our thoughts and feelings. Mental health and social well-being are considered to be taboo, and we tend to forget how important it is to take care of ourselves, not only physically, but mentally and emotionally.

Through these blog posts, I hope to inspire others to also take the time and write down their feelings. Their proudest accomplishments. Their biggest regrets. By the time you're too old to write another word, you'll have all the memories you've saved to look back on.
